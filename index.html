Matthew Delia Variation Navigation

The attached pages on this site are various variations that Matthew Delia made in the Web Art and design class at Temple University to a piece of dialogue from the script The Deal which he also wrote. The piece of dialogue, which is a small exchange between the characters of Blake Scottman and his girlfriend Maria, discusses the aspects of time and the impact time has on them.
